# Leaf-Disease-Detection-using-CNN
Leaf diseases significantly threaten global agriculture by reducing crop yield and quality, leading to economic losses and food insecurity.

Using Python programming and libraries such as TensorFlow and Keras, the project processes images of healthy and diseased leaves to train a CNN model capable of recognizing multiple disease types accurately.

The dataset consists of four disease categories:

Bacterial Blight – Characterized by dark, water-soaked lesions starting at leaf tips or edges, spreading rapidly.

Blast – Causes gray-white lesions with brown margins primarily on leaves at all growth stages.

Tungro – Viral disease-causing stunted growth, yellow to orange discoloration, and mottling of leaves.

Brown Spot – Presents as small to large spindle-shaped brown lesions with yellow halos on leaves, often merging to form necrotic areas.
